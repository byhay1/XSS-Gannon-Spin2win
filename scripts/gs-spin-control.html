What Was Fixed

The previous version had issues with tick calculation that caused the wheel to only land on positions 1 and 2. Here's what was fixed:

  • Correct rotation calculation: The formula now properly accounts for the wheel's starting position and marker placement
  • Section-based targeting: Instead of ticks, we now target specific sections (1-8)
  • Landing mode options: Choose between exact landing or slightly randomized landing within a section
  • Proper offset calculation: The rotation now correctly positions the selected section at the marker
